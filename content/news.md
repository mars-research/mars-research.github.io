  ([pdf](/doc/2023-kisv-atmo.pdf))

  **[ACSAC'23](https://www.openconf.org/acsac2023/modules/request.php?module=oc_program&action=program.php&p=program)**

  ([pdf](/doc/2023-hotos-kernel-interfaces.pdf))
